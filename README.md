# About
Pitlane Predictions is a Formula 1 prediction style fantasy game where you predict the results of the upcoming Grand Prix weekend and compete with friends to earn points based on correct predictions.  

Featuring personalized leagues with fully customizable scoring categories and difficulty-based bonus points, every league offers a unique competitive experience tailored to its participants.

Powered by the OpenF1 public API, race results and standings are updated automatically in real time throughout the season.
